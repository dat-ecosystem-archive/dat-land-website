## <span class="color-green">dat.land</span> applications & tools

#### A Community-Driven Ecosystem

<span class="color-green">dat://</span> is a peer-to-peer protocol for building decentralized tools. Is is a community-driven project powering a next-generation Web. Building on the design of Bittorrent, Git, and other technologies, Dat creates new possibilities for data-rich applications. View **[applications](/apps)** and **[modules](/modules)**.
